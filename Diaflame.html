Diaflame
is a smart cooktop which understands you and your cooking.
Challenge constraint
  • Gas based
  • Fixed cooktop
  • Should have IOT features
Why?
Cleaning the cooktop is a painpoint for the people
The cooktop has a camera for assisting cooking. It detects the base size of utensil and opens the surface accordingly
Iris mechanism helps in achiving the flat surface of the cooktop
One can interact using the touch interface and slider for adjusting the flame and opening diameter. Also one can adjust time to cook.
